Lesley Anderson

UX/UI Designer & Front End Developer
Vancouver BC Canada


As a UX/UI Designer & Front End Developer I specialize in the User. From interfaces to experiences my goal is to create things that work effortlessly and intuitively. I rapidly iterate designs by sketching wireframes and building clickable prototypes. I design mobile first & responsive using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JQuery to do it.

About me

In 2009 I received a BFA from Emily Carr University and worked with artists and publishers to create original prints at a collaborative print studio in Vancouver, B.C. from 2009 to 2012

While completing an MFA from Concordia Uniersity in 2012-2015 I was a teaching assistant and part time professor working with artists to distill their interests as I deepened my own practice.

Post MFA, I found a passion for UX design and front-end development, studying them both at BrainStation in Vancouver BC.

When I'm not creating beautiful solutions, painting in my studio, or cycling out and about, I'm probably playing with my puppy
